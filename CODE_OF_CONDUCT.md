# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep this project respectful, technically focused, and constructive.

Please:

- engage in good-faith technical discussion
- critique ideas, proposals, and implementations rather than people
- avoid harassment, abuse, discrimination, and personal attacks
- keep feedback direct, specific, and professional

## Scope

This applies to project spaces, including:

- issues
- pull requests
- discussions
- review comments

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that do not meet this standard.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact:

- `conduct@fide.work`
